About me: my name's Matt Lubel and I am an aspiring full-stack web developer! I studied mathematics at university, and have always been fascinated by understanding how things work. To me, the web is an amazing and vast place full of wonders to explore. I'm currently part of the great Coding for Everyone London community- you can usually find me working on my javascript skills and hanging out with loads of other inspiring people! I built this site from scratch with HTML, CSS and pure js (see the source)- I love clean design but my main interest is in sorting out the back-end logic. My plans for the near future are to try to become a part of the amazing Founder and Coders initiative in London or Nazareth, to learn nodejs and be at the forefront of modern web development, and be in a position to give back to the community- either through making useful apps, working with social enterprises or simply helping the amazing communities of willing coders around the world!

Here are some of my projects, including those I'm currently working on: They are all open-source, please check out the READMEs and feel free to comment/contribute!
